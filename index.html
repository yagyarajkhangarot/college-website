Prestige Institute of Management and research

Prestige Institute of Management and Research is a B-School and an Educational Institute located in Indore city of Madhya Pradesh, India. It offers education in disciplines such as MBA, BBA and B.Com and is a private institute specializing in Management Education.Indore is a well -established premier management Institute of Madhya Pradesh. The institute is known as Best Upcoming Law College in Central India 2018 by CEGR (Center for Education Growth and Research at India International Center New Delhi). The college is popular for its MBA program (both full-time and part-time) with placements. Other than this, the college offers BBA, B.Com, BJMC, LLB, LLM, etc. Admission to all courses is either merit-based or entrance-based.
